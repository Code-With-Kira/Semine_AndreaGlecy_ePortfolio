Case Studies

Taste Haven, a new restaurant, needed a stylish and functional website to showcase their menu and attract more customers. My role was to design and develop a responsive website that highlighted their brand identity while making reservations simple and convenient. I created a modern layout with warm visuals, an interactive menu, and an integrated table booking system, all optimized for speed and mobile use. As a result, the website launch increased online reservations by 35% within two months and strengthened the restaurant’s digital presence.

Case Studies

Folderly is a free academic organizer app designed to help students manage tasks, courses, and study materials. I was responsible for designing a minimalist and intuitive interface that worked seamlessly on both iOS and Android. To achieve this, I built a clean UI featuring task lists, course folders, schedules, and widgets, along with added functions like calendar sync and a personalized student ID card. After launch, the improved design increased student retention by 40%, and users praised the app’s simplicity and effectiveness in organizing their academic life.
